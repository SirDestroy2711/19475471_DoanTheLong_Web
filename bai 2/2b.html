One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by Jhon Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becoms acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of thehomeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great books. It is well written and interesting. Other books by Jhon Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
